# Product Manifesto

Version: 1.0
Status: Approved

## Mission

MoneyPulse helps users understand their daily spending capacity.

## Promise

Know tomorrow. Decide today.

## Values

- Clarity
- Calm
- Trust
- Action
- Simplicity

## Product Direction

MoneyPulse is built around practical answers, not complex reports.

The first experience must show what matters today.

The product should reduce confusion and make everyday choices easier.
