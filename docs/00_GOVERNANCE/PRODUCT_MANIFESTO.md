# Product Manifesto

Version: 1.0
Status: Approved
Owner: MoneyPulse Leadership

## Mission

MoneyPulse helps people make better financial decisions before they spend, not after the damage is done.

## Product Promise

Know tomorrow. Decide today.

## Why MoneyPulse Exists

Most personal finance tools describe the past. MoneyPulse must help the user act in the present.

People do not need more dashboards. They need a calm answer to a hard question:

How much can I safely spend today without hurting tomorrow?

## User Outcome

MoneyPulse should reduce anxiety, increase clarity, and support confident daily decisions.

The product succeeds when a user can open it, understand their situation in seconds, and act with less doubt.

## Values

- Clarity over complexity.
- Calm over alarm.
- Trust over engagement tricks.
- Action over observation.
- Consistency over novelty.

## What We Build

MoneyPulse is decision intelligence for personal finance.

It combines current cash position, known commitments, near-term obligations, and user goals into a simple recommendation that can be explained.

## What We Do Not Build

- A trading app.
- A spreadsheet replacement.
- A social network for finance.
- A generic analytics dashboard with no decision value.

## Product Direction

The first screen must answer what matters today.

The second layer must help the user test a purchase before they buy.

Every additional feature must strengthen one of these outcomes:

- daily spending clarity;
- pre-purchase confidence;
- goal-aware planning;
- explainable improvement suggestions.

## Standard for Shipping

We only ship features that are understandable, explainable, and useful on mobile in real life moments.
